By Groom

It feels really weird to write this as I have never owned a diary in my life. However since this is a once in a lifetime experience for the happiest day of my life, I told Nadia “Okay, I’m down for this”. So to start, I’m going to be 26 years old this year. In fact, Nadia and I were born on the same month as our wedding month. I am the eldest out of three siblings with a brother and a sister who are currently studying in university.

Family has always been (and always will) be my priority and nothing can top that. Being the eldest in the family and having an age gap of 6 & 7 years apart with my siblings definitely sparked a higher sense of protectiveness and attention towards them in me.

Other than that, I value friendship very much. I enjoyed having several close friends back in school, but I truly learnt the value of friendship when I enrolled into SASER after PMR. Here, we went through the hustle and hardships together during our 2 years in school. They make me believe the saying “High school friends are your lifelong friends”. They are the ones who can just click, talk and laugh together even though you have not met them after some time. These are my closest circle that I will forever treasure.

Then I enrolled into UNITEN for my overseas prep and met a bunch of good friends and Nadia (I will describe more on our meeting in the next section). I pursued my study in Drake University (Drake) starting in August 2011 and was joined by Nadia a year later. I would say that I had a roller coaster journey here but it is a memorable one. Now that I am done with my studies, I started my career journey in EY and have been stuck here ever since.

I am a huge football fan and Liverpool is my favourite team. Currently, I am trying to keep up with a healthy lifestyle. Well it’s because I gained 25 kg in a span of 4 years during my time in Drake (yes I know it’s crazy, that is why i need to be super dedicated in keeping up with a healthy lifestyle). It is a good thing that Nadia is also together with me in this journey to be healthier.
